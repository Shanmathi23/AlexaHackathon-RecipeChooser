Stir together oil and next 6 ingredients until well blended.
If necessary, remove giblets and neck from chickens, and reserve for another use. Rinse chickens with cold water; pat dry.
Loosen and lift skin from chicken breasts with fingers (do not totally detach skin).
Rub 2 Tbsp. olive oil mixture evenly underneath skin of each chicken. Carefully replace skin. Rub remaining olive oil mixture over both chickens, coating evenly.
Place chickens, side by side, on a lightly greased wire rack in a pan.
Bake at 425° for 30 minutes; cover loosely with aluminum foil, and bake 45 to 55 minutes or until a meat thermometer inserted in thickest portion of breast registers 165°.
Let stand 15 minutes before slicing.
